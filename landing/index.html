Common questions.

When does Flamora launch?
We're targeting Q3 2026. Waitlist members get access 1 week before public launch.
Is it free?
Yes — core tracking is free forever. Flamora Pro adds advanced FIRE simulators and tax-aware projections. Early waitlist users get Pro for life.
Does it work outside the US?
Yes. Flamora supports US, UK, EU, CA, AU, JP, CN, IN, SG, HK currencies and bank connections via Plaid (where supported).
How do you connect to my bank?
Through Plaid — read-only access. We never see your password. We can't move money. We just see balances and transactions.
Will my partner see my data?
Only if you invite them. Flamora is single-user by default. Couple's mode is on the roadmap.
What if I don't want to connect a bank?
Manual entry mode is supported. You'll input balances yourself, and Flamora calculates the rest.
